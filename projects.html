UX Designer for an Informatics 191 Project to restructure behind the scenes work. Sep 2017 - Present

Description: Our project for Infomatics 191 with Professor Ziv and Professor Deneburg as our sponsers. Informatcs 191 and Informatics 117 are project based courses where a team of students are assigned to a sponser to help create a system. The students benefit from this by getting work exprience they can put on a resume while the sponsers gets a system. However, the work that the profesors to keep this running is quite strenuous and in some cases nedlessly complicated. This is where we come in. The Project is to create a website to rework how students and sponsers submit information to the professors and automatically organize this information for ease of understanding. Currently there is only a prototype website and the project will continue next quarter.
