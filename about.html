About me

My name is Paul Nguyen, and I was born to immigrants from Vietnam. My father is a big inspiration as he is currently an engineer at a company. And he did it all in spite of the fact that he didn’t come with much when he came to this country. So, I’m inspired by him to do my best and be just as successful as him. I graduated from Irvine High school and graduated from UCI in April with a major in Informatics and HCI. I may not be the best at what I do but I am passionate about UX design and passionated about helping people with my skills.

My UX Philosophy

UX design and Human computer interactions is something that is not appreciated and in some cases ignored and over looked. Which is both a shame and will probably be a major problem in the future. From class I saw how things can go wrong, like people getting burned by x-rays, bridges falling apart, and terrible housing ideas. And most of these problems could have been averted with a little HCI and common sense. I wanted to become a UX designer to help avert theses disasters while also just making it easier for people to use technology. With technology getting more advanced and more complicated it can make life so much better, but it will all be useless if people can’t use them, so I want to make things easier for everyone, so things can get better.

My Interests
